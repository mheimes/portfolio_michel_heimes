Blender
Hotkeys
Grab item:
  • Press G: Grab item
  • Press G → X: Grab item and move on x-axis
  • Press G → Y: Grab item and move on y-axis
  • Press G → Z: Grab item and move on z-axis
Rotate item:
  • Press R: Rotate item
  • Press R → X: Rotate item on x-axis
  • Press R → Y: Rotate item on y-axis
  • Press R → Z: Rotate item on z-axis
Scale item:
  • Press S: Scale item
  • Press S → X: Scale item on x-axis
  • Press S → Y: Scale item on y-axis
  • Press S → Z: Scale item on z-axis
Zoom-In/ -Out:

Entweder/ Oder:

  • Klassisches Zoomen mit zwei Fingern (Zoom Movement)
  • Control + Zwei Finger (Hold + Drag)
Move on screen-map:
  • Shift + two Fingers (movement in different directions)
Quick Favorites:
  • Press Q → Frame selected: Focus on the currently clicked item (item, camera, light)
Orientate item/ view certain side quickly:
  • 1: Front view
  • ⌘1: Back view
  • 3: Right view
  • ⌘3: Left view
  • 7: Top view
  • ⌘7: Bottom view
    • Switch between Orthographic view and Perspective view
      • Orthographic view: → Methode of representing three-dimensional objects as two-dimensional “drawings”. Perspective is ignored, so that parallel lines in the scene also appear parallel in the viewport. (No depth in scene)
      • Perspective view: → In 3D graphics software such as Blender the perspective view is one way a 3D artist can view and manipulate objects in the scene. It helps the artist see how the final image should appear when rendered with realistic camera lens optics.
      • Switch between these modes: 5
Edit Mode → Edit object (show/ edit Vertecis - little dots around the object)

Entweder/ Oder:

  • Click “Tab” → changes “Object Mode” to “Edit Mode”
  • Change the mode in the upper left corner
